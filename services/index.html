How it works

  1. Meet: focused call to gather details, links, and context about the issue(s) you’re facing.
  2. Clarify: I map what’s happening, what’s blocking progress, and what data is needed.
  3. Follow-up: written summary with priorities and actionable next steps.
  4. Escalate (if needed): if support is slow or not tracking the problem, I can liaise with contacts at distributors and DSPs to get the issue in front of the right team.
