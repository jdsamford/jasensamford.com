Background

I’ve spent nearly a decade helping independent artists navigate distribution, metadata, royalties, and catalog issues — first inside DistroKid, where I built the Artist Relations, Education, and Support teams, and now through my independent consulting practice.

I’ve worked with thousands of artists and labels at every scale: new artists uploading their first track, managers cleaning up messy catalogs, and platforms building new creator tools and policies.

What I do now

  • Distribution audits and catalog cleanup
  • Metadata conflict resolution (ISRC, UPC, ownership)
  • Royalty routing, reporting, and troubleshooting
  • Streaming-fraud prevention and risk assessment
  • Artist-facing education and documentation
  • Team training for labels and music companies

Approach

My work is direct, practical, and built around what artists actually deal with: mismatched ISRCs, duplicated releases, missing royalties, incorrect credits, support stalls, and confusing DSP responses.

I focus on clarity first — mapping what's happening, what caused it, and what needs to happen next, whether that’s fixing metadata, escalating through my network, or helping you build a repeatable process.
