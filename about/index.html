Who I am

I help artists, labels, and music companies make sense of distribution, royalties, and metadata. I focus on clear systems, clean data, and practical steps you can use right away.

Before consulting, I spent almost ten years at DistroKid supporting artists and building internal systems for scale.

What I do

  • Release planning and distribution strategy.
  • Metadata hygiene (ISRC/UPC), catalog cleanup, conflict resolution.
  • Royalty setup, audits, and troubleshooting.
  • Streaming-fraud prevention and best practices.
  • Team training and process design for artist services.

How it works

  1. Meet: focused call to gather details and context.
  2. Clarify: map what’s happening and what data is needed.
  3. Follow-up: summary with priorities and next steps.
