About Jasen Samford

Music distribution consultant for independent artists, labels, and music companies.

Who I am

I help artists, labels, and music companies make sense of distribution, royalties, and metadata. I focus on clear systems, clean data, and practical steps you can use right away.

Before consulting, I spent almost ten years at DistroKid, joining early and helping it grow from a startup into the world’s largest distributor. I built and led artist support and education, partnered with Berklee College of Music, KEXP, and Folk Alliance, and helped artists move from first upload to final payout.

What I do

  • Release planning and distribution strategy.
  • Metadata hygiene (ISRC/UPC), catalog cleanup, conflict resolution.
  • Royalty setup, audits, and troubleshooting.
  • Streaming fraud prevention and best practices.
  • Team training and process design for artist services.

How it works

  1. Meet: focused call to gather details, links, and context.
  2. Clarify: map what’s happening, what’s blocking progress, and what data is needed.
  3. Follow-up: short written summary with priorities and actionable next steps.

When support is slow or not tracking the problem, I can work with contacts at distributors and DSPs to get the issue in front of the right team.

Background

  • Partnerships: Berklee, KEXP, Folk Alliance.
