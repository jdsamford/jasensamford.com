Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

Jasen Samford Consulting ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we use it.

Information We Collect

Analytics Data

We use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-focused analytics tool. Plausible does not use cookies and does not collect personal data. It tracks:

  • Page views and referral sources
  • Country-level location (not precise location)
  • Device type and browser

This data is aggregated and cannot identify you personally.

Advertising

We use Google Ads conversion tracking to measure the effectiveness of our advertising. This may use cookies to track conversions when you interact with our ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising through Google's Ad Settings.

Booking Sessions

When you book a session through Calendly, you provide your name and email directly to Calendly. Please review Calendly's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle your information. We use this information solely to conduct our scheduled consultation.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Understand how visitors use our website
  • Measure advertising effectiveness
  • Conduct booked consulting sessions
  • Improve our services

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies:

  • Plausible Analytics: No cookies used
  • Google Ads: May set cookies for conversion tracking

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect functionality on other websites.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Plausible Analytics (privacy-focused analytics)
  • Google Ads (advertising)
  • Calendly (scheduling)

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

Contact

For privacy-related questions, email jasen@jasensamford.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
