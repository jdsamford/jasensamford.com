About Jasen Samford

Music distribution consultant for independent artists, labels, and music companies.

Who I am

I’m Jasen Samford, a music industry consultant who helps artists, labels, and music companies make sense of distribution, royalties, and metadata. I focus on clarity, clean data, simple systems, and practical steps that help music reach audiences faster and more reliably.

Before launching my consulting practice, I spent nearly a decade at DistroKid, joining as one of the first employees and helping it grow from a startup into the world’s largest distributor. I built and led the artist support and education teams, partnered with Berklee College of Music, KEXP, and Folk Alliance, and helped artists navigate releases from first upload to final payout.

What I do

  • Release planning and distribution strategy
  • Metadata, ISRC/UPC hygiene, and catalog cleanup
  • Royalty setup, audits, and troubleshooting
  • Streaming fraud prevention and best practices
  • Team training and process design for artist services

Why it helps

Most problems in distribution come from unclear ownership, messy metadata, or weak planning. I help fix that early so royalties go where they should, releases hit deadlines, and nothing gets lost in the process.

Background

  • Scaled artist support from early stage to millions of users
  • Partnered with Berklee, KEXP, and Folk Alliance on education and outreach
