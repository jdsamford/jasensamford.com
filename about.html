About Jasen Samford

Music distribution consultant for independent artists, labels, and music companies.

Who I am

I help artists and teams release smarter, fix metadata problems, and get royalties where they should go. I focus on clear systems, accurate data, and practical steps you can use right away.

Before consulting, I spent nearly ten years at DistroKid, joining early and helping it scale from startup to the world’s largest music distributor. I built and led artist support and education, partnered with schools and festivals, and worked across product, ops, and policy to solve real artist problems.

What I do

  • Release planning and distribution strategy
  • Metadata, ISRC/UPC hygiene, and catalog clean‑up
  • Royalties and reporting: setup, audits, and issue triage
  • Streaming fraud risk checks and best practices
  • Team training and process design for artist services

Why it helps

Most issues are avoidable with better planning and clean data. I make distribution transparent, efficient, and fair—so you can spend more time on the music.

Credibility

  • Former Director of Education at DistroKid
  • Scaled artist support from early stage to serving millions
  • Partnerships with Berklee College of Music, KEXP, Folk Alliance
